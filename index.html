Hot Cocoa Recipe

Warm up with this simple recipe:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Optional: Marshmallows

Heat the milk, stir in the cocoa and sugar, and enjoy!
